Boarding

In addition to our daycare services, we offer overnight accomodations! Your furry friend will receive dinner and an evening snack before retiring to their private luxury room.

Check-in time for overnight guests:

3:00 - 4:00 pm for some play time with the daycare - or -
5:30 - 6:30 pm for dinner and lite play with the other boarding guests.

We are happy to schedule check-in by appointment!

We also offer kitty boarding!
